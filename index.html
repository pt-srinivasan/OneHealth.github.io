Welcome to OneHealth

We seek to to address is inefficiencies, security vulnerabilities, and the need for interoperability of our current Electronic Health Record (EHR) systems. These issues have often led to inaccurate patient data, hindered communication between providers, compromised patient data, and incorrect diagnoses. The implementation of a universal Electronic Health Record system with the use of blockchain technology hopes to correct these issues and transform healthcare across the country.

Our Solution

As of now, there is no universal EHR system, which causes slowdowns between healthcare providers as well as issues in the emergency room when a patient's EHR can't be accessed. However, with a Universal EHR system, we need to ensure that the data is accessible, but only to those who have permission.
